CivAdjective defines the descriptive name of the civ. This can be determined by completing this sentence: "I am _____ who leads the _____ which is populated by the _____ people."  The last blank would be your value for CivAdjective.


EG: I am Chief Two-Dogs who leads the Kingdom of Ghent which is populated by the Ghentese people.
